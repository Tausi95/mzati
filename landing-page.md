## DESIGN NOTES FOR DEVELOPER

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Bold, large, dark charcoal (#1a1a2e or similar)
- Body: Regular weight, muted grey (#555 or similar)
- Accent: Warm terracotta (#c9623f) OR deep teal (#2a7f7f) — pick one, use consistently

**Colors:**
- Background: Warm white (#fafaf8) or light sand (#f5f0eb)
- Dark sections: Deep navy (#1a2332) or charcoal (#1f1f1f)
- Accent / CTA: Terracotta or teal (consistent throughout)

**Spacing:**
- Generous padding on all sections (min 80px top/bottom)
- Clean whitespace — let content breathe

**Sections Order:**
1. Hero
2. The Problem
3. The Opportunity
4. Strategy (3 Pillars)
5. Deliverables
6. KPIs
7. Timeline
8. Pricing
9. Why This Works
10. Final CTA

**Components needed:**
- Button (primary, with hover state)
- Card (for pillars, KPIs)
- Stat block (for KPIs)
- Timeline block (3-column)
- Pricing tier (strikethrough + highlight)
- Section labels (small caps, accent color)
